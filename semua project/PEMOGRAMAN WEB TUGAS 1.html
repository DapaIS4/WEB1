What's is UNAI

Uneversitas advent indonesia (UNAI) is a Higher Education institution managed by the Seventh-day Adventist Church of the Western Union of indonesia. The history of UNAI is that was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding School der Advent Zending. In the past, this school was founded for the education of the evangelist of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.


Univesitas Advent Indonsia has 6 faculties

1. Faculty of Philosophy
2.Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
3.Faculty of Economy
4.Faculty of Nursing
5.Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
6. Faculty of information Technology
